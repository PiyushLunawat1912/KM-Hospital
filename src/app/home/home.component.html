About Us

At K.M.M Hospital, we believe that healthcare is not just about curing illnessesâ€”it's about caring for people with compassion, dedication, and excellence. Our journey is a testament to passion, hard work, and a commitment to improving lives.

  • Our Story

    The foundation of K.M.M Hospital is built on the vision of Dr. Santosh Lunawat, whose passion for serving the community sparked the beginning of our journey. In 2010, Dr. Lunawat started his medical practice with an allopathy clinic, where he provided dedicated healthcare services for over six years, earning the trust and gratitude of his patients. Driven by his dream to expand access to quality healthcare, he founded K.M.M Hospital in January 2016, starting with a 15-bed facility. What began as a small hospital with a big vision has since grown into a leading healthcare institution, known for its patient-first approach and comprehensive medical services.

  • Our Mission

    To provide exceptional healthcare services with compassion, integrity, and excellence. We are committed to delivering patient-centered care through modern medical practices and advanced technologies.

  • Our Vision

    To be a trusted healthcare provider in the community, continually expanding our services and enhancing patient outcomes through innovation and expertise.

  • Looking Ahead

    As we continue to grow, our commitment to the community remains steadfast. K.M.M Hospital is not just a healthcare provider; we are a family dedicated to supporting you through every step of your health journey. With innovation, excellence, and care at our core, we look forward to serving you for years to come. At K.M.M Hospital, your health is our mission, and your care is our promise.
